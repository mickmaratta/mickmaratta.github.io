About Me

My name is Mick and I’m a front-end developer and professional musician from Toronto, Canada. I went to school for Fine Arts at York University and graduated with honours in Jazz and Contemporary performance. Although my first passion was music I have always been interested in web development; building my first websites on geocities. Over the years I have created many websites for the different musicians and bands I’ve worked with. More recently I started to study web development to gain a better understanding of different technologies such as React, Redux, Bootstrap, Javascript, and others. If you have a project or are interested in working together, please reach out.
